We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

We use the information you submit solely to:

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or manage an active client relationship. If no engagement follows, we do not retain your data beyond a reasonable response window.

You have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, email us at nilansh2929@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store submission data on our own servers — it is delivered directly to our inbox via Formspree's secure infrastructure.
